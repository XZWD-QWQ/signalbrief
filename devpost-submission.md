# SignalBrief Devpost Submission Draft

## Project Name

SignalBrief

## Elevator Pitch

An AI tool that turns messy user feedback into clear product opportunities, MVP priorities, and a one-page product brief.

## About The Project

## Inspiration

Product builders often receive feedback from many messy sources: user interviews, support tickets, app reviews, surveys, sales notes, and team discussions. The hard part is not collecting feedback, but turning it into clear product decisions.

SignalBrief was created to help makers, founders, and product teams move from scattered feedback to focused action.

## What It Does

SignalBrief is an AI-assisted product thinking tool. Users paste messy user feedback into the app, and SignalBrief generates:

- Pain point clusters
- Key product opportunity areas
- MVP priorities
- User stories
- A concise one-page product brief

The goal is to help people understand what users are really asking for and decide what to build next.

## How We Built It

We built SignalBrief as a lightweight web app focused on one clear workflow: feedback in, product decision brief out. The prototype uses a structured analysis flow to detect feedback themes, rank product opportunities, and format the results into a shareable brief.

## Challenges

The biggest challenge was designing output that feels useful instead of generic. Product builders do not just need summaries; they need evidence, priorities, and next steps.

## What We Learned

This project helped us practice product thinking, AI-assisted prototyping, and rapid shipping. We learned how important it is to keep the workflow narrow enough that a first-time user can understand the value in one click.

## What's Next

Next, we plan to connect a live AI model, improve evidence extraction, add export options, and use Novus.ai analytics to understand where users engage or drop off.

## Built With

AI, Product Management, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Novus.ai, Vercel
